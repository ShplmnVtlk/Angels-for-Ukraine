Our Supporter Promise

ABOUT HOW WE USE YOUR DONATIONS

We spend donations only on moving Ukrainian residents to safety:

  • from Ukraine to other EU countries,
  • from war zones to relatively safe regions in Ukraine
  • wounded in the war from Ukraine to Poland, where they will continue their journey to medical facilities in other countries.
